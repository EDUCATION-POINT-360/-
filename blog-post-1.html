Top 5 Study Techniques That Actually Work

Learn smart, not hard — these strategies will boost your performance.

1. Pomodoro Technique: Work in 25-minute bursts followed by 5-minute breaks. Boosts focus and avoids burnout.

2. Active Recall: Force yourself to retrieve information instead of passively reading.

3. Spaced Repetition: Review material at intervals to store it in long-term memory.

4. Mind Mapping: Visual connections help understand complex concepts better.

5. Teach What You Learn: Explaining topics to others solidifies your understanding.
