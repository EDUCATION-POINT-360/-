How to Stay Motivated During Exams

Simple ways to keep your energy and confidence up!

1. Break Big Tasks: Divide chapters or goals into small, doable parts.

2. Reward Yourself: After each study session, give yourself something to enjoy — snacks, music, a short break.

3. Study With Friends: Group study keeps you accountable and can be more fun.

4. Visualize Success: Remind yourself of your goals and what success will bring.

5. Rest Well: Never underestimate the power of proper sleep and breaks!
