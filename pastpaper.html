About Education Point

Bridging the gap in Digital Learning.

Education Point is a digital resource ecosystem founded with a single mission: to empower students with high-quality, verified academic content. We understand that the difference between an average result and a top position is often the access to the right practice materials.

By centralizing years of board exam data, we allow students to bypass the "search struggle" and go straight to learning. Our platform is meticulously organized to serve the Matric and Intermediate student community across all major boards.
